Industry Overview

HR Activities in IT & ITES Industries

With emerging technologies, IT and ITES industries have achieved greater targets over the years. But with this growth came challenges in monotonous management operations — shift planning, workforce engagement, time tracking, and more. These repetitive tasks divert focus away from primary business goals. EKTA HR reduces these hurdles so your teams can build, ship, and grow.

Challenges & Solutions

IT Industry Challenges — Solved by EKTA HR

Each challenge the IT sector faces has a uniquely crafted solution built into EKTA HR.

Managing Flexible Working Hours

Developers, designers, and team members operate on flexible schedules, making manual attendance reconciliation time-consuming and error-prone for HR and managers.

✦ EKTA HR Solution

Flexible Shift Configuration: Configure flexi-shifts where employees complete total required hours on their own schedule — without following a fixed clock-in/out. EKTA HR auto-reconciles all data for payroll.

Tracking Employees' Time & Tasks

IT companies run multiple concurrent projects. Manually tracking time spent per task per team member for client billing and productivity reporting was difficult and inaccurate.

✦ EKTA HR Solution

Timesheet Management: Managers can list all tasks per employee per day. The timesheet reviews total time per task, measures productivity, and maintains a complete record for client projects.

Managing Information of New Recruits

Manual onboarding required HR to collect, store, and maintain employee information in paper files or spreadsheets — insecure, time-consuming, and difficult to search or audit.

✦ EKTA HR Solution

Online Employee Onboarding: Employees add, update, and manage their own information and documents on a secure centralized platform, eliminating HR's manual collection effort entirely.

Measuring Employees' Performance

Setting team objectives, tracking progress, and storing performance data manually in spreadsheets was tedious and made it hard to act on insights or spot top performers early.

✦ EKTA HR Solution

Intuitive Performance OKR System: Create and assign custom OKRs with different rating techniques. Review progress regularly and download detailed review reports to analyze targets achieved and improvement areas.

Lack of Engagement in Workforce

In distributed and remote work environments, isolation grew and genuine interaction dropped. Sharing company updates and maintaining morale across teams became increasingly difficult.

✦ EKTA HR Solution

Robust Workforce Engagement: EKTA HR's ESS portal and mobile app enable company-wide communication. Leadership can share updates instantly with all employees, and AI mood surveys help HR identify and improve team wellbeing.

Managing Attendance Manually

The industry needed a contactless, hygienic attendance system that worked for both on-site employees and traveling/remote staff — without biometric devices or manual data sharing.

✦ EKTA HR Solution

Mobile-Driven Geo-Fence Attendance: EKTA HR's geo-fencing lets employees mark attendance via mobile without biometrics. Off-site travel staff data is captured and stored centrally for seamless payroll integration.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does EKTA HR handle flexible working hours for developers?
EKTA HR's flexi-shift configuration lets you define a daily hour target for each employee rather than fixed clock-in times. Employees complete their hours within a configurable window, and the system auto-reconciles all records for attendance and payroll — no manual tracking needed.
Can managers track time spent on specific client projects?
Yes. EKTA HR's timesheet module allows managers to assign tasks to employees linked to specific projects. Time logs are captured per task, and managers can generate project-wise reports for client billing, productivity analysis, and delivery tracking.
How does EKTA HR improve remote workforce engagement?
EKTA HR's ESS portal and mobile app keep your distributed teams connected. Company-wide announcements, policy updates, and HR communications reach every employee instantly. Built-in mood surveys help HR identify disengagement early and act before it escalates.
Is geo-fencing suitable for remote and hybrid IT teams?
Yes. EKTA HR's geo-fencing allows employees to mark attendance from any location via mobile — whether at office, client site, or working remotely. All data syncs centrally in real time, eliminating the need for physical biometric devices.
Can EKTA HR handle performance reviews for engineering teams?
Absolutely. The OKR-based performance module lets you create custom objectives and key results per team or individual, assign rating scales, set review cycles, and generate downloadable reports — all within the EKTA HR platform without needing separate tools.
