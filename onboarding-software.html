Common Questions

Onboarding Solution FAQs

What is Onboarding Software?

It’s technology that assists employers to facilitate a flawless transition for new hires — featuring a digital dashboard to track progress and communicate with talent teams.

How long should the onboarding process take?

With EKTA HR, it takes just a few minutes to upload employee data and a maximum of a week to fully integrate a new hire into the organization culture.

What are the benefits of an online system?

Saves time and money by going paperless, ensures data security via cloud storage, and guarantees accuracy and compliance in every hire.
