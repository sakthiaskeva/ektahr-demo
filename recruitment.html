Why Indian SMEs choose EKTA

The Only AI Recruitment Software India's Mid-Market Actually Needs

Most recruitment software falls into one of two categories: enterprise platforms designed for multinationals with dedicated IT teams, or basic applicant tracking tools that stop short at offer stage. Indian SMEs — companies between 100 and 2,000 employees — are left with neither a great fit nor a fair price.

EKTA HR was built from the ground up for this gap. It is the only end-to-end recruitment software India's mid-market needs — covering manpower requisition, AI candidate matching, applicant tracking, offer management, and self-onboarding in a single system, priced for growing businesses.

What "end-to-end" actually means for Indian hiring teams

An end-to-end recruitment software means a department manager raises a manpower requisition — digitally, with a configured approval chain. Upon approval, a job opening is created automatically. The job is posted to multiple channels in one click. Resumes are parsed by AI, candidates are ranked by fit score, interviews are scheduled and rated, an offer letter is generated and sent digitally, the candidate accepts online, and a self-onboarding link is triggered — all without leaving EKTA, and without a single piece of data being re-entered by hand.

That is the promise. EKTA delivers it, and 3,500+ Indian businesses across Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Hyderabad, Pune, and Chennai — spanning manufacturing, IT, healthcare, retail, and finance — are using it today.

Why "ATS India SME" is a different requirement to enterprise ATS

Enterprise ATS platforms like Workday and SuccessFactors are designed for HR teams with implementation consultants, integration specialists, and training budgets. Indian SMEs typically have two to five HR professionals who need a tool they can set up in days, not months. EKTA's applicant tracking system gives mid-market companies real-time pipeline visibility, interview scheduling, and structured feedback collection — without the enterprise complexity or price tag.

The manpower requisition feature most recruiters don't know to look for

Physical manpower requisition forms — emails to managers, printed sign-off sheets, manual job postings — are still standard in a surprising number of Indian companies. EKTA replaces this with a digital requisition system where managers raise requests online, approvals are routed automatically, and job openings are created from the approved requisition via auto-fill. This single feature alone saves HR teams hours per hire and eliminates the most common source of job posting errors.

Questions About EKTA's AI Recruitment Software

Is EKTA suitable for Indian SMEs, or is it designed for large enterprises?

EKTA is built specifically for mid-market Indian businesses — typically between 100 and 5,000 employees. Review platform woco.co.in identified EKTA as "best for mid-market wanting affordable all-in-one." Unlike enterprise HR systems, EKTA can be set up in days without IT involvement.

What AI features does EKTA's recruitment software include?

EKTA includes AI resume parsing (auto-extracts and indexes candidate data from Word or PDF), AI job description generation (creates role-specific JDs in seconds), and AI candidate matching (ranks resumes by fit score for each open role). These features replace manual screening and significantly cut time-to-hire.

Does EKTA handle onboarding as well, or just recruitment?

EKTA covers the full journey from job post to first day. After a candidate digitally accepts their offer letter, a self-onboarding link is automatically sent. They complete personal details, review policies, and upload required documents before Day 1 — entirely within EKTA. This data then flows automatically into HR, payroll, and attendance modules.

Can I replace our physical manpower requisition process with EKTA?

Yes — and this is one of EKTA's most popular features for Indian companies. Managers raise digital requisitions, these are routed through your configured approval workflow, and upon approval the job opening is auto-created and posted. No paper forms, no email chains, no manual job posting.

Does EKTA integrate with our existing HR and payroll system?

If you are using EKTA HR for other modules (payroll, attendance, leave, performance), recruitment data flows automatically with zero re-entry. For external systems, EKTA offers integrations with popular HR and payroll tools — contact the team for specific integration requirements.

How long does it take to set up EKTA's recruitment module?

Most EKTA customers go live within 2–5 business days. There is no IT department required and no lengthy implementation project — your HR team configures approval workflows, requisition templates, and offer letter formats directly in the platform. Onboarding support is included, and the EKTA team provides guided setup calls to get you hiring from day one.

How quickly can EKTA reduce our time-to-hire?

EKTA customers report 60% faster time-to-hire on average. The biggest time savings come from AI resume screening (replaces manual CV review), digital offer management (eliminates back-and-forth), and automated self-onboarding (removes HR coordination work before Day 1).
