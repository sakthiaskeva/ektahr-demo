FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Superannuation?
Superannuation is a pension plan designed to provide financial security to employees after retirement. Contributions are made by the employer to a trust fund, often managed via LIC or similar entities.
How does the software handle LIC funds?
Our software allows scheme-wise contribution uploading, LIC interest upload facility, and tracking of LIC annuities, providing a complete 360-degree view of your LIC fund status.
Can employees apply for settlements online?
Yes, through the ESS portal or mobile application, employees can submit applications for settlement, which are then automatically routed through your trust's approval workflow.
Does it provide Investment Yield tracking?
Absolutely. The system provides real-time yield analysis and a mark-to-market facility to help trust managers optimize their investment portfolios and maximize returns for members.
