Mobile FAQs

Everything You Need to Know

What is a Selfie Punch?

It’s a secure way for employees to mark attendance by clicking a live photo from their phones. Integrated geotagging ensures they are in the correct location, eliminating identity theft.

Can I declare Income Tax through the app?

Yes! The app allows for easy investment declarations. Submit tax-saving proofs, specify assets, and provide HRA details for transparent processing.

Does it support offline geo-punching?

Absolutely. EKTA's advanced mobile solution provides tools for offline geo-punching, ensuring connectivity issues don't hinder accurate record-keeping.
