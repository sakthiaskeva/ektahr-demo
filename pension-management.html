FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pension Management Software?
Pension management software helps organizations automate the administration of retirement benefits, including pension eligibility tracking, disbursement calculations, investment monitoring, and compliance reporting.
How does it integrate with existing HRMS?
EKTA HR's Pension Software seamlessly integrates with our core HR modules, allowing for automated data flow of employee details, salary history, and contribution records.
Can trust members track their own benefits?
Yes, through our dedicated Employee Self-Service (ESS) portal and mobile app, members can check their balances, download statements, and track their pension status in real-time.
Does it handle statutory compliance?
Absolutely. Our software is designed to stay up-to-date with latest regulations, automatically generating required reports and ensuring all disbursements are compliant with current laws.
