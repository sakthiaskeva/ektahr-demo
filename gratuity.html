FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gratuity Management Software?
Gratuity management software helps organizations automate the calculation, tracking, and disbursement of gratuity benefits, ensuring accuracy and compliance with labor laws.
Does it support Actuarial Valuation?
Yes, EKTA HR provides built-in tools for actuarial reporting and valuation, helping you maintain a clear view of your future liabilities and funding status.
Can I customize calculation formulas?
Absolutely. Our calculation engine allows you to define custom formulas based on your organization's policies or state-specific government regulations.
How secure is the fund management?
We use a Maker-Checker authorization workflow for all financial transactions, ensuring that every movement of funds is verified and authorized by at least two people.
