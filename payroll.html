Payroll FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is automated payroll software secure?

Yes, EKTA HR uses end-to-end encryption and SOC Type 2-certified cloud data centers to safeguard all compensation and banking information.

Can I customize my payslip format?

Absolutely. Choose from over 15 different payslip layouts and sections that best fit your organizational branding and needs.

Yes, the system keeps track of retroactive changes in attendance or revisions and automatically calculates the pending payments and statutory deductions.
