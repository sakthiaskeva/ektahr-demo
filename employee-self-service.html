FAQ

Common Questions About EKTA's ESS Portal

Everything Indian HR teams and employees ask before switching to a self-service portal.

What is an Employee Self Service (ESS) portal?

An ESS portal is a web-based tool that allows employees to manage their own HR, payroll, and benefits data — applying for leave, downloading payslips, regularising attendance — without contacting HR directly. EKTA's ESS portal is built specifically for Indian businesses, covering Indian leave types, Form 16, PF, ESI, and professional tax.

How does Form 16 download work in EKTA's ESS portal?

Once TDS filing is complete, EKTA automatically generates Form 16 and makes it available for download in each employee's dashboard. No waiting for HR to email it — employees access it the moment it's ready. This is compliant with the Income Tax Act requirements for Form 16 issuance.

What Indian leave types does EKTA ESS support?

EKTA supports all standard Indian leave types: Casual Leave (CL), Privilege/Earned Leave (PL), Sick Leave (SL), Compensatory Off, and Maternity/Paternity Leave. You can also configure custom leave policies per your organisation. State-wise public holiday calendars are built in — no manual setup required.

Is attendance regularisation linked to payroll?

Yes. Every approved attendance regularisation in EKTA ESS is automatically synced to the payroll module. LOP (Loss of Pay) is re-calculated without manual HR intervention — so your payroll is always accurate, even after late regularisations are approved.

What is the price of ESS software in India?

EKTA's Employee Self Service software starts at ₹49 per employee per month. New customers get 3 months free with no credit card required. The pricing is per-seat and transparent — no hidden implementation fees. View full pricing at ektahr.com/pricing-plan.html.

Can employees view PF and ESI contributions in the portal?

Yes. Real-time PF contribution statements (under the EPF Act 1952) and ESI deduction summaries are visible every month alongside the payslip — fully transparent and downloadable. Employees don't need to log into the EPFO portal separately for their contribution history.

How does IT investment declaration work in EKTA ESS?

Employees can submit IT investment proofs — LIC, PPF, HRA, home loan, NPS, and other Section 80C/80D instruments — directly through the portal. Tax is auto-computed once declarations are submitted. No physical forms, no scanning, no repeated HR follow-ups during declaration season.

Is EKTA's ESS portal accessible on mobile?

Yes — fully. EKTA's ESS portal is available as a dedicated mobile app (Android and iOS) and is also fully responsive on mobile browsers. Employees can download payslips, apply for leave, regularise attendance, and view PF statements from their phone without needing a desktop.
