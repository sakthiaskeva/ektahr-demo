Portal FAQs

Common Questions

What is an Employee Self-Service (ESS) Portal?

It’s a web-based tool allowing employees to manage their own HR, payroll, and benefits data independently, reducing the need for constant HR interaction.

Is my personal and financial data secure?

Yes, EKTA HR uses banking-level encryption and unique login credentials for every employee, ensuring that sensitive data is only visible to authorized users.

Can I access the portal on my mobile phone?

Absolutely. Our portal is fully responsive and also available as a dedicated mobile app, ensuring you can manage your HR tasks on the go.
