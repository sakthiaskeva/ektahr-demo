Industry Overview

Role of HR in the Automotive Industry

As the automotive sector expands, managing workforce operations grows increasingly complex. From salary structures and shift management to travel reimbursements, HR teams face daily challenges that require purpose-built digital solutions.

Pain Points

HR Problems Encountered in Automotive

Addressing these challenges before they accumulate is essential for unhindered business performance.

Separate Attendance for All Units

With multiple production units across locations, tracking and reconciling attendance across different wage cycles was cumbersome and time-consuming.

Managing Changing Shifts

Multiple rotating shifts across assembly lines are nearly impossible to assign manually, causing workflow delays and attendance inconsistencies.

Manual Approval Process

Employees submitting travel claims via email required HR to manually verify, forward, and process reimbursements — a slow and error-prone approach.

Multiple CTC Structures

Different employee grades carry different CTC components, making manual salary computation at scale burdensome and delay-prone for payroll teams.

Manual Onboarding

Collecting PAN, Aadhaar, employment history, and other details manually from each new hire is tedious and creates backlogs for HR teams.

Multiple Attendance Cycles

Separate working hours for managers and floor workers create multiple attendance cycles that are difficult to reconcile accurately during payroll runs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can one system manage attendance across all our plant locations?
Yes. EKTA HR's geo-fencing allows you to define separate attendance zones for each plant. All data is automatically reconciled to a central server, giving you a single unified view across all units.
Does it support complex rotating shift patterns?
Absolutely. You can define unlimited shift types — including morning, evening, night, and rotating rosters. The system auto-calculates night shift allowances and OT during payroll processing.
Can we handle different CTC structures for workers and managers?
Yes. EKTA HR supports unlimited salary templates. You can configure different CTC components, variable components, and deductions for each employee grade, and run payroll for all in a single cycle.
Is the travel expense approval process fully digital?
Yes. Employees submit advance and expense claims through the mobile app with supporting attachments. Managers approve digitally, and data is pushed to finance automatically for clean reimbursement cycles.
