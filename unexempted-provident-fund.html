FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Unexempted Provident Fund?
Unexempted PF refers to establishments that do not have their own PF trust and are directly covered by the EPFO. The employer and employee contributions are deposited with the EPFO.
How does EKTA HR help with ECR?
Our system records monthly contributions and generates the ECR file in the exact format required by the EPFO portal, eliminating manual spreadsheet errors.
Can employees update KYC via mobile app?
Yes, our mobile application allows employees to view their KYC status and upload/update details like UAN and Aadhar, which are then routed to HR for approval.
Does it handle International Workers?
Yes, EKTA HR supports Statement IW generation and compliance tracking for International Workers, ensuring your global workforce is managed correctly.
