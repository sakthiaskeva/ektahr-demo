Everything about EKTA HR
Software in India

HR software (also called HRMS software) is a digital platform that automates core human resource functions — from recruitment and onboarding to payroll, statutory compliance, attendance, and performance management. Indian businesses specifically benefit from HR software with built-in PF, ESI, PT, and TDS compliance, which eliminates the risk of manual errors and compliance penalties under India's complex labour law framework.
Yes. EKTA HR is one of the few HR software in India built with full statutory compliance automation out of the box. Here is what is covered automatically:

Provident Fund (PF) — EPFO: EKTA HR auto-calculates employee and employer PF contributions per the Employees' Provident Fund & Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952, governed by the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO). ECR (Electronic Challan cum Return) is generated automatically. UAN (Universal Account Number) management, bulk UAN generation, and PF transfer requests are all handled within the platform. Every EPFO circular update — wage ceiling revisions, administrative charge changes — is applied automatically.

ESI — ESIC: Contributions to the Employees' State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) under the ESI Act, 1948 are auto-computed based on the current gross salary threshold (₹21,000/month). Monthly ESIC challans, half-yearly returns, and IP (Insured Person) registration are handled inside EKTA HR. Every ESIC notification on rate changes is applied without manual intervention.

Professional Tax, TDS, and Labour Welfare Fund are similarly automated with state-wise slab updates. EKTA HR is fully aligned with India's Four Labour Codes reform framework issued by the Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India — covering the Code on Wages, the Industrial Relations Code, the Code on Social Security, and the Occupational Safety Code.
EKTA HR has a dedicated compliance update mechanism that monitors regulatory changes from three primary government bodies:

EPFO (Employees' Provident Fund Organisation) — under the Ministry of Labour and Employment, EPFO periodically revises PF wage ceilings, administrative charges, and ECR filing formats. EKTA HR's compliance team tracks every EPFO circular and pushes updates to the platform before the effective date — so your payroll always computes the correct PF contribution without any manual changes on your end.

ESIC (Employees' State Insurance Corporation) — ESIC revises ESI contribution rates, salary thresholds, and return filing schedules periodically. EKTA HR monitors every ESIC notification and updates eligibility checks, contribution percentages, and challan formats automatically.

Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India — India's Four Labour Codes (Code on Wages, Industrial Relations Code, Social Security Code, Occupational Safety Code) are being implemented in phases across states. EKTA HR's platform is designed to accommodate the Labour Code framework, including the revised definition of "wages" that impacts PF, ESI, and gratuity calculations.

When the government changes the rules, EKTA HR updates the software — so you never pay a compliance penalty for an outdated rate or missed filing.
