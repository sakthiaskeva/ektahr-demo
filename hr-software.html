FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EKTA HR software.

What is HR Software?

HR software is a digital solution that automates core HR functions — from recruitment and onboarding to payroll, performance management, and offboarding — enabling organisations to manage their workforce efficiently and reduce manual work.

What reports are available?

Daily attendance reports, absence reports, payroll reports, performance reports, recruitment reports, and comprehensive employee information reports — all exportable in multiple formats.

Is there a Mobile App?

Yes! EKTA HR has a mobile app for Android and iOS users, functioning as an Employee Self-Service app for attendance tracking, leave management, payslip access, and expense submission.

Can employees access payslips and policies?

Employees can access and download their payslips and company policies digitally through the ESS portal — available any time, on any device.

Can I migrate from spreadsheets or another HR software?

Yes. Our implementation team assists with full data migration from spreadsheets or any existing HR software — with proper mapping, validation, and a structured onboarding plan.

How long does implementation take?

Implementation timelines depend on company size and complexity. Small businesses typically go live in 2–4 weeks, while larger organisations with complex configurations may take 6–12 weeks.

Do you support PF/ESI/PT and TDS automatically?

Yes. EKTA HR automates all key statutory deductions — PF, ESI, PT, and TDS — with the latest slab rates, ensuring accurate payroll calculations and full legal compliance.

Can we integrate biometric devices and accounting tools?

Yes. EKTA HR supports integration with biometric attendance devices and popular accounting applications including Tally and QuickBooks for seamless end-to-end data flow.
