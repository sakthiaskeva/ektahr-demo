Anonymous feedback option

Respondents stay confidential managers, peers, and subordinates give honest feedback without fear of repercussion. Research shows anonymous 360° feedback yields 35% more candid responses than named reviews.

Continuous real-time feedback

Enable anyone in the organisation to give or receive continuous feedback in real time not just at the end of a review cycle. Regular communication helps take corrective action before small issues become performance problems.

Perception gap analysis

Compare self-review scores with manager and peer scores to surface alignment issues instantly. EKTA HR's perception gap analysis gives HR leaders a clear view of reality vs. expectations before it affects team performance.

1:1 meeting tracker for managers

Log objectives, action items, and discussion points from every manager-employee meeting. Searchable meeting records keep everyone accountable and help managers track progress between formal review cycles.
