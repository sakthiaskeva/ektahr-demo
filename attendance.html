Common Questions

Attendance Solution FAQs

What is an Attendance Management System?

It's a digital solution to register an employee's presence or absence according to their in-out timings, directly impacting work hours and project productivity.

Does it integrate with existing biometric hardware?

Yes, EKTA HR seamlessly integrates with major hardware like ESSL, ZKTeco, and Matrix via database, IP, or SDK connectivity.

Can employees regularize their missed punches?

Absolutely. Employees can apply for correction through the web portal or mobile app, which then routes to their manager for approval.

How does geo-fencing attendance software work in India?

EKTA HR's geo-fencing attendance software lets HR define virtual perimeters around any work location. Employees can only mark attendance when their device GPS is within the boundary — preventing proxy and out-of-location punches. It works for offices, factories, warehouses, and distributed field teams across India.

Is EKTA HR a reliable facial recognition attendance system for Indian businesses?

Yes. EKTA HR's facial recognition attendance system uses AI-based liveness detection to prevent photo spoofing, matching each employee's face to their registered profile in under a second. It is compliant with Indian data protection guidelines and does not store raw images.

Does the attendance software offer payroll integration?

EKTA HR is attendance software with built-in payroll integration. Attendance data — present days, LOP, overtime, comp-off — flows directly into the payroll engine without manual data entry. It also supports integration with third-party payroll tools like GreytHR, Keka, and Zoho Payroll.

What makes EKTA HR a good mobile attendance app for employees in India?

The EKTA HR mobile attendance app for employees supports geo-tagged clock-in, selfie punch, facial recognition, and offline sync — all from a single app available on iOS and Android. Employees can also apply for leave, view shifts, regularise punches, and check payslips from the same app.

Can EKTA HR handle complex shift management for multi-location businesses?

Yes. EKTA HR is designed as full-featured shift management software for businesses with auto, fixed, and rotational shifts. You can manage roster patterns by department, allow shift swap requests, and configure different overtime and attendance policies per shift type — all from a single admin dashboard.
