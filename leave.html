Common Questions

Leave Solution FAQs

What leave types does EKTA HR support?

You can configure unlimited leave types including sick, vacation, personal, maternity, and custom compensatory leaves with individual policies for each.

How does it ensure compliance with regulations?

The system automatically accounts for statutory entitlements and sends notifications to both employees and admins to ensure adherence to labor laws.

Is there a mobile app for managers to approve leave?

Yes, the EKTA mobile app allows managers to review, approve, or reject leave requests and check team availability on the go.
