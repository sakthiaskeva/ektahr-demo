Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the right time for a startup to invest in HR software?
The ideal time is when administrative burdens grow beyond spreadsheet manageability, typically around 15-20 employees. Adopting EKTA HR early ensures standardized processes and compliance scaling seamlessly from day one.
How does HR software help startups save time and costs?
It drastically reduces manual effort required for payroll processing, attendance tracking, and compliance filings (like PF/ESI). By preventing calculation errors and compliance penalties, it keeps overhead lean and protects capital.
Is the software secure enough for our employee and IP data?
Absolutely. EKTA HR is secured with AES encryption, strict SOC-compliant cloud protocols, role-based access controls, and full adherence to regional data protection laws, keeping your proprietary and personnel data locked down.
Can we manage flexible working hours and remote developers?
Yes. EKTA HR integrates a flexi-shift policy system and mobile geo-fenced attendance. Remote and hybrid workers can log time accurately while you maintain visibility over daily productivity.
Is this affordable for bootstrapped or early-stage startups?
Yes, we offer exclusively tailored, flexible per-user SaaS pricing models. You only pay for what you use, without heavy upfront implementation or hidden licensing fees.
