LMS FAQs

Learning Solution Questions

What is an Online Learning Management System?

LMS is a platform enabling workforce to improve skills through a digital medium, featuring SCORM support and integration with performance management systems.

Does it support multiple media types for courses?

Yes, you can upload videos, PDFs, and interactive SCORM packages to create a diverse and engaging training library.

Can managers track progress of their team members?

Absolutely. Managers have access to real-time dashboards showing course completion rates, assessment scores, and training status for their entire team.
