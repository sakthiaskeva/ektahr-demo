Frequently Asked Questions

What is HR & Payroll Software for the Healthcare Industry?
It is software that helps hospitals and healthcare organizations manage employee records, payroll, attendance, shifts, and compliance in one place. It simplifies workforce management while ensuring accurate pay and adherence to healthcare labor regulations.
Why do Healthcare Organizations Need Specialized HR Software?
Healthcare organizations face industry-specific challenges such as managing complex shift patterns, tracking compliance credentials, and maintaining hygiene with contactless attendance. General HR software cannot handle this — specialized HRMS can.
How does HRMS Software Manage Shift-Based and 24×7 Healthcare Staff?
EKTA HR provides roster management and a team leave calendar that lets HR plan shifts efficiently and staff know in advance if colleagues are absent. Shift allowances are automatically integrated with payroll so there are zero manual adjustments.
How does HRMS Track Attendance for Doctors, Nurses, and Support Staff?
EKTA HR tracks attendance via AI-powered face recognition for hygiene-sensitive environments and biometric device integration. Geo-fencing tracks field and home-care staff while auto-approval workflows handle leave requests without manual HR intervention.
Can Healthcare Organizations Automate Salary Calculations and Payslips?
Yes. EKTA HR automatically calculates salaries including overtime, night-shift allowances, and incentives. Digital payslips are auto-generated and distributed to every employee, eliminating manual computation entirely.
Is EKTA HR suitable for Small Clinics and Single-Doctor Setups?
Yes. EKTA HR offers flexible plans suited for clinics of all sizes — from single-location clinics with a handful of staff to multi-speciality hospital groups with thousands of employees.
