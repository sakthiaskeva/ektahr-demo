Industry Challenges

Overcome the Day-to-Day HR Struggles in Hospitality

Struggles with Seasonal Workforce

Hospitality businesses frequently scale their workforce based on seasons, events, and occupancy levels. With staff constantly joining, changing roles, or moving across locations, keeping track of attendance, compliance, and day-to-day HR tasks becomes overwhelming.

How EKTA HR Helps:
  • Centralized employee management
  • Real-time attendance and shift tracking
  • Employee self-service for smoother operations
  • Integrated payroll within a complete HR system

Difficulties in Maintaining Compliance

Staying compliant with local labor laws, minimum wage regulations, and statutory deductions is critical but complex when manually managing a diverse hospitality workforce.

How EKTA HR Helps:
  • Automated compliance calculations
  • Compliance-ready statutory reports
  • Significant error reduction and penalty prevention

Challenges of High Employee Turnover

Hospitality teams see frequent employee movement. Managing records, onboarding, role changes, and sudden exits can quickly become disorganized without the right tools.

How EKTA HR Helps:
  • Accurate and secure employee records
  • Easy onboarding and rapid exit management
  • Integrated payroll automation for Full & Final settlement

Complexities in 24/7 Shift Scheduling

Manually calculating night shifts, weekend differentials, split shifts, and overtime across different hotel departments leads to payroll errors and disgruntled staff.

How EKTA HR Helps:
  • Support for multiple payroll groups
  • Real-time shift and attendance tracking
  • Automated overtime and shift allowance calculation

Multi-Location Performance Management

Tracking employee performance across hotels, food outlets, or franchised properties often lacks consistency. Managers struggle to align teams when data is spread out.

How EKTA HR Helps:
  • Centralized performance management system
  • Standardized review cycles and OKRs
  • Real-time insights and multi-property reporting

Managing Travel and Expense Claims

Hospitality managers and corporate teams frequently travel for training, audits, and inter-property coordination. Manual expense tracking causes delays and policy violations.

How EKTA HR Helps:
  • Digital travel and expense management
  • Policy-based expense controls and limits
  • Faster approval workflows and reimbursements

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HR Software for Hospitality?
HR software for hospitality is a digital platform designed to manage employee tasks like recruitment, scheduling, payroll, and compliance in hotels, restaurants, and similar businesses grappling with high staff turnover and shift work.
What are the Must-Have Features?
Essential features include shift and attendance tracking for rotational duties, payroll automation with overtime capabilities, bulk onboarding modules for seasonal hiring, and multi-location dashboards for hotel or restaurant chains.
Can it handle shift-based, rotational, and night-duty staff?
Yes, EKTA HR supports flexible shift management, attendance tracking, and automatic payroll adjustments for rotational, split, and night shifts — which are critical in the hospitality industry.
Can I manage multiple hotel properties from a single dashboard?
Yes, our centralized dashboard allows you to handle multi-location operations, segregate employee data, apply local compliance rules, and pull consolidated reporting across all your properties and franchises.
Does it support bulk hiring for the hospitality industry?
Yes, the software includes applicant tracking, automated workflows, and digital document collection specifically designed for high-volume seasonal hiring and fast onboarding.
